About the GreenBot Project

The University of Luxembourg, in partnership together with the Pall Center, founded this project which has for objective of promoting ethical and eco-responsible awareness to the consumers during their groceries shopping experience. This system, in the form of a virtual shopping companion for customers, should allow to scan a visual reference in the package of product, such as a QR code, to inform users about the ethical and eco-friendly attributes of the scanned product.

The app is a pilot study which provides ecological and ethical product information to consumers who shop at the Pall Center. The purpose of our study is to examine if such a practical and educational tool is useful to consumers, and if their shopping behaviour changes during the study. Detailed information about the project is on our website.

This website evaluates and interprets the shopping data such that other members of the study can analyse them.
